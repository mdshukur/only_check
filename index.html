Yearn Finance Global
Accelerator Program

Yearn Finance aims to encourage and empower blockchain start-ups and projects to build, grow and foster innovation across the decentralized technology ecosystem. We will be launching an accelerator program to encourage and assist startups and developers looking to build innovative solutions that allow us to bring Blockchain to 3 billion users by 2023. We want to help use blockchain to break down barriers and create a world where everyone creates and exchanges value transparently, efficiently, and securely.

With support from our Accelerator partners, the Yearn Accelerator program provides selected projects with end-to-end support across the wide spectrum of strategy formulation, go-to-market execution, and subject matter guidance across technological mentorship, token economics, marketing, and fundraising. The startups and projects selected benefit from working with a variety of esteemed, distinguished, and experienced program mentors. The Accelerator is looking for projects focused on DeFi, NFTs, infrastructure, DAOs, and gaming. We are flexible with respect to stage, asset type, and geography.

Overview

The Yearn Global Accelerator is a ten-week accelerator. The program provides hands-on support and mentorship from blockchain thought leaders as well as workshops across 5 key modules:
  • Engineering and Product Development,
  • Product Design,
  • Governance,
  • Tokenomics and Go-To-Market Strategy,
  • Fundraising and Community Building

The program will culminate with a Demo Day where selected projects will have the opportunity to present to a global community.

Each team selected to participate will be granted seed funding from Yearn Finance. Additional funding of up may be offered to teams displaying exceptional potential and performance.

The Accelerator program will be a collective initiative between Yearn Finance, Polychain Capital, Impossible Finance, and key industry partners to be announced soon.
